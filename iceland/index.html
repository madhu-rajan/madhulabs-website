🌑 Eclipse Preparation — Aug 12, 5:46 PM

Camera Gear Checklist

  • Nikon D7000 body + spare battery (×3-4)
  • Nikkor 28-300mm — primary eclipse lens (450mm equiv)
  • Nikkor 18-200mm — wide landscape shot (totality over Búðakirkja)
  • Baader AstroSolar 5.0 film — cut to fit 28-300mm, rubber-band mount
  • Eclipse glasses ×4 (ISO 12312-2 certified)
  • Remote shutter (Nikon MC-DC2) or use 2-sec timer
  • Sturdy tripod + 2nd small tripod or GorillaPod
  • Extra SD cards (shoot RAW/NEF — brackets eat space)
  • Lens cloth (sea spray at Budir!)

Eclipse Day Tips
Scout Day 2: Walk around Búðakirkja on arrival (Aug 11) and find your exact tripod position. Sun at 5:46 PM will be ~25° altitude in the SW sky. Use PhotoPills app to confirm framing.
Weather backup: If clouds roll in, drive to Stykkishólmur (~45 min east) or Borgarnes (~1.5h south). Keep the car ready and monitor weather apps.
Totality is 1m 30s. Shoot brackets for 30 seconds, then put the camera down and LOOK.
